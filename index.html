How to Play

  1. Click or tap on a card to flip it over.
  2. Flip another card to find its matching picture.
  3. If the two cards match, they stay open.
  4. If they don’t match, they flip back after a moment.
  5. Keep matching pairs until all cards are open.
  6. Try to finish in the shortest time and with the fewest moves!

Tip: You can change the difficulty from Easy to Hard using the menu above.
